An amazing math game for exercising your math skills!

Math Play is a fun app that helps you improve your skills in math. It presents you with mathematical questions based on the operation of your choice. The more questions you answer correctly, the more challenging the questions become. And when you've answered enough questions, you have to provide your answers before the timer runs out! This app can be a great help whether you want to improve your math skills for school, work or life in general.
