Improve your spelling in English with this app

This app aims to help you improve your spelling in English. It presents you with a set of words, and you have to choose the correct spelling. At anytime, you can go back to review the mistakes. Keep track of how many correct answers you got, and how many wrong answers. The more you keep playing, the better you get in spelling words.
